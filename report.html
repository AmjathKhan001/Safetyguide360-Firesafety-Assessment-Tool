30-Day Fire Safety Action Plan

A structured, immediate plan to help you address the most significant risks.

1

Week 1: Immediate Actions

Focus on **critical life-safety and escape routes**.

  • Clear all clutter from stairwells, corridors, and primary exit doors.
  • Ensure all fire extinguishers (if present) are unexpired, visible, and accessible.
  • Establish and practice a basic family escape plan (two routes out, meeting point).
2

Week 2: Equipment & Installation

Focus on installing **detection and first-aid fire fighting equipment**.

  • Install functional **Smoke Detectors** in your residence.
  • Purchase and place a small, domestic **Fire Extinguisher** (ABC type) in your home.
  • Install a **Fire Blanket** near the kitchen stove for cooking fires.
3

Week 3: Training & Awareness

Focus on **resident education and communication**.

  • Organize a brief meeting with neighbors to discuss common area safety (obstructions).
  • Educate all household members on the basic **PASS** method for using an extinguisher.
  • Identify and confirm the route and distance to the nearest fire station.
4

Week 4: Review & Maintenance

Focus on **system checks and long-term planning**.

  • Check the expiry date of all fire fighting equipment.
  • If applicable, request a maintenance check of the building's fire pump or hydrant system.
  • Plan for an annual fire safety audit/training session.
